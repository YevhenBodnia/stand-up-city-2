Concept

This year we are all facing heavy times and it’s even harder to get through with the comedy clubs closed! Where do we turn to for comic relief and how the heck do comics get a paid gig?

Stand Up City is the place where people can turn to for good laughs, great stories, and the chance to discover new comics without paying general admission. On the same time it is the place where comics can do what they do best while getting paid and a chance at winning a couple grand.

Show
details

In season 1 of Stand Up City comedians will have 30 minutes an episode to perform written or improvised material while recording themselves from the comfort of wherever! The subject matter is open and creativity is encouraged to go far, above, and beyond.

The video will be streamed through TH3 NUM6ERS website as well as video platforms such as YouTube and Vimeo. The audio will stream through TH3 NUM6ERS podcast on Apple, Spotify, and all platforms streaming podcast.

Equipment/
Editing provided

What do comics
do?

  • 30 mins of standard stand up or small skits in the comforts of your home or wherever (keep in mind that the video content will also be converted into audio alone);
  • Turn in a time stamp of parts you’d like highlighted from your act;
  • Send in finished content within a week of receiving equipment.

Compensation

$250 for each comic who completes the gig.

$2,500 for the favorite set of the season based on twitter votes.
